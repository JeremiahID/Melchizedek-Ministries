Who We Are

A church is a group of people who are, in word, together. We're together with God beacuse of his kindness and commitment to humanity,together as a family learning to embody this kindness to one another and beyond into the wider world. All this is only possible through Jesus. the one who brings us together and keep us together. At Melchizedek Ministries Worldwide, we believe in core beliefs of the church, such as the authority of the Bible, salvation through Jesus Christ. These beliefs form the foundation of our teachings and guide our community in faith and practice.

Mission & Vision

At Melchizedek Ministries Worldwide we have a mission to create an atmosphere where the presence of the lord can dwell and a Vision that lords shall be raise from thebody of christ for God's kingdom leardership.
